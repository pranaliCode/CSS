Environment

The environment is everything around us, such as air, water, land, plants, animals, and people. It helps all living things survive. We get fresh air to breathe, clean water to drink, and food from nature. A clean environment keeps us healthy and happy. We should protect the environment by planting trees, saving water, reducing pollution, and keeping our surroundings clean. It is our responsibility to take care of the environment for a better future.

We should also be aware of the impact of our actions on the environment. Simple steps like recycling, using energy-efficient appliances, and reducing waste can make a big difference. By working together, we can create a sustainable environment for ourselves and future generations.
